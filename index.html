Find Your Hat

Welcome to "Find Your Hat"!

Your goal is to navigate through the field and find your hat:

Winning Condition: Reach the hat without falling into any holes or going out of bounds.
Losing Condition: You lose if you fall into a hole or if you move out of the field's borders.

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character . As you move, footprints will mark your path.
